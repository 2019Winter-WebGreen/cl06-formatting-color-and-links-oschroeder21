Shih Tzu's Are The Best Bud's!

And This Is Why That Is True...

A Shihtzu is a very small dog! They are hypoallergenic (they have hair instead of fur!) meaning they do not shed and no ones allergic!

Most of the time these cute things are dog friendly, and people friendly. They need a lot of love and time because they have health issues, and a lot of grooming needs for thier fabulous hair.

Show dogs that are Shih Tzu's usually grow out their locks, but most people shave it down because the hair is a lot to take care of!

Though, overall these dogs have a lot to offer and will give you a all the love!
